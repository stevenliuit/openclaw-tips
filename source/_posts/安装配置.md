<h2>安装方法</h2><h3>Shell安装（推荐）</h3><p>curl -fsSL https://openclaw.ai/install.sh | bash</p><h3>Windows PowerShell</h3><p>iwr -useb https://openclaw.ai/install.ps1 | iex</p><h3>npm全局安装</h3><p>npm install -g openclaw@latest</p><h3>pnpm全局安装</h3><p>pnpm add -g openclaw@latest</p><h2>Docker安装</h2><p>参见/ install/docker</p><h2>从源代码安装</h2><p>git clone https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw.git</p><p>cd openclaw</p><p>pnpm install</p><p>pnpm build</p><h2>配置文件位置</h2><ul><li>默认：~/.openclaw/openclaw.json</li><li>可通过OPENCLAW_CONFIG_PATH环境变量指定</li></ul><h2>目录结构</h2><ul><li>~/.openclaw/ — 主配置目录</li><li>~/.openclaw/workspace/ — 智能体工作区</li><li>~/.openclaw/skills/ — 本地Skills</li><li>~/.openclaw/agents/ — 智能体配置</li></ul><h2>环境变量</h2><ul><li>OPENCLAW_CONFIG_PATH — 配置文件路径</li><li>OPENCLAW_GATEWAY_PORT — Gateway端口</li><li>OPENCLAW_GATEWAY_TOKEN — Gateway认证令牌</li></ul>
